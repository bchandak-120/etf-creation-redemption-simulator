🚀 Portfolio Website Setup

⚠️ Important: Since Python isn't installed on your system, you'll need to open the portfolio file directly in your browser.

📁 Step 1: Locate Your Portfolio File

Your portfolio website is in the file named: index.html

It's in the same folder as this file: my-portfolio

🌐 Step 2: Open Your Portfolio

Choose ONE of these methods:

Method A - Double Click (Easiest)

  1. Open File Explorer
  2. Navigate to: C:\Users\chand\OneDrive\Desktop\New folder (2)\CascadeProjects\windsurf-project\my-portfolio\
  3. Double-click on index.html
  4. Your portfolio will open in your default browser

Method B - Drag & Drop

  1. Open your web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, etc.)
  2. Drag the index.html file into the browser window
  3. Your portfolio will load immediately

Method C - Right Click

  1. Right-click on index.html
  2. Select "Open with" → Choose your browser
  3. Your portfolio will open

📝 Step 3: Add Your Content

Once your portfolio is open:

  • 📄 Resume: Click the upload area to add your resume PDF
  • 📚 Research Papers: Click upload area to add papers
  • 💼 Projects: Click "Add Project" to showcase your work
  • 🎯 Experience: Click "Add Experience" with reflections

⚙️ Step 4: Customize

To personalize your portfolio:

  • Right-click on index.html → "Open with" → Notepad or VS Code
  • Find "Your Name" and replace with your actual name
  • Update your professional title and social links
  • Save the file and refresh your browser
🎯 Open My Portfolio Now
💡 Pro Tip: Bookmark your portfolio once opened for easy access later!
